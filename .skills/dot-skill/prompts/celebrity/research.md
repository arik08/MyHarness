# Celebrity Research Prompt

Research across six dimensions:

1. Writings and first-person material.
2. Conversations and interviews.
3. Expression DNA.
4. Decisions and tradeoffs.
5. External views and criticism.
6. Timeline and evolution.

For each source record:

- Title.
- URL or local path.
- Source type.
- Date if available.
- Relevant paraphrased findings.
- Confidence.

Prefer primary and long-form sources. Avoid generic profile pages, copied biographies, content farms, and unsourced summaries.
